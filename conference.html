Conference Overview

Bharat AI AI Innovation Conference 2026 brings together India's leading AI researchers, industry experts, policymakers, and innovators for two days of intensive learning, networking, and collaboration. With expert speakers across 10+ focused tracks, this is India's most forward-thinking AI conference launching in 2026.

From generative AI and responsible AI to healthcare, fintech, and agriculture — every dimension of AI's transformative potential is explored, debated, and celebrated.
