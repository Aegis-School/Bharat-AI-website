FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Bharat AI Innovation 2026

▸ What is Bharat AI Innovation 2026?

Bharat AI Innovation 2026 is a leading AI conference and exhibition taking place on June 2-3, 2026 at World Trade Center Mumbai. It brings together top speakers, exhibitors, and AI professionals to explore cutting-edge topics in artificial intelligence, machine learning, GenAI, and enterprise AI solutions.

▸ What topics are covered at the AI conference?

The conference features 10+ specialized tracks including Generative AI (GenAI), Large Language Models (LLMs), AI for Healthcare, FinTech AI, Manufacturing AI, AgriTech innovations, Cloud & Infrastructure, Enterprise AI Solutions, AI Startups, and AI Research. We also have dedicated sessions on ChatGPT, AI chatbot development, LLM fine-tuning, and practical AI implementations.

▸ Who should attend this AI summit?

This event is ideal for AI researchers, data scientists, machine learning engineers, startup founders, enterprise leaders, CTOs, technology managers, investors, policymakers, students, and anyone interested in artificial intelligence and its applications. Whether you're exploring AI for your business or advancing your technical skills, this conference offers valuable insights and networking opportunities.

▸ What are the ticket prices for the conference?

We offer five pass types: Visitor Pass (Free) for exhibition and keynote access, Academic Pass (INR 999) for students and researchers, Delegate Pass (INR 4,999) for full conference and exhibition access including sessions and materials, Media Pass (By Invite) for accredited journalists and media partners, and VIP Pass (INR 14,999) for premium benefits including VIP lounge, priority seating, exclusive VIP dinner, and speaker meet & greet.

▸ How can I exhibit at the AI exhibition?

We offer 7 exhibition booth types to suit every budget: Startup Pod (1.8 sqm, ₹35,000 + GST), Explorer Booth (4 sqm, ₹84,000 + GST), Innovator Booth (6 sqm, ₹92,400 + GST), Accelerator Booth (8 sqm, ₹1,56,000 + GST), Enterprise Booth (12 sqm, ₹2,20,000 + GST), Flagship Pavilion (22.5 sqm, ₹6,24,000 + GST), and Mega Pavilion (56.25 sqm, ₹15,00,000 + GST). 132 total booth spaces across 977 sqm of exhibition area. All packages include exhibitor passes, booth infrastructure, and marketing benefits. Contact us for custom packages.

▸ Are there training workshops available?

Yes! We offer hands-on training workshops led by industry experts covering GenAI, Agentic AI, VibeCoding, AI Product Development, Recommender System Development, AI Chatbot Development, LLM Model Fine-Tuning, and more. These practical sessions provide real-world skills you can immediately apply to your projects.

▸ Can startups pitch at the conference?

Absolutely! Our Innovation Stage provides startups with 8-minute pitch slots to showcase their AI innovations to investors, enterprise decision-makers, and industry leaders. This is an excellent opportunity to gain visibility, receive expert feedback, and connect with potential partners and customers.

▸ Where is the conference venue located?

The event takes place at World Trade Center Mumbai, Cuffe Parade, Mumbai, Maharashtra. The venue is centrally located with excellent connectivity, close to luxury hotels, and offers world-class conference facilities with stunning waterfront views.
